I am an assistant professor in the math department of the University of Georgia .

Previously I was a Phillip Griffiths Research Assistant Professor at math department of Duke University working with Samit Dasgupta. I did my PhD at the math department of University of Wisconsin, Madison. My advisor is Melanie Matchett Wood.

I study number theory from both the algebraic side and the analytic side.
