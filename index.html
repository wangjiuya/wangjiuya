I am an assistant professor in the math department of the University of Georgia .

Previously I was a graduate student from the math department of University of Wisconsin, Madison. My advisor is Melanie Matchett Wood.

My research interest is on number theory from both the algebraic side and the analytic side. My research also involves representation theory and group theory.
